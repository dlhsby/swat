# Getting to Know the Dashboard

After signing in, you land on the **Dashboard** — a summary of the current state of
waste hauling and levy.

![SWAT dashboard](/img/web/dashboard.png)

## Parts of the screen

- **Top bar** — search, notifications, and the account menu (avatar) to open
  **Settings** (light/dark theme & language) and to sign out.
- **Left sidebar** — the main navigation, grouped into Monitoring, Master Data, Hauling,
  and Users & Access. Click the collapse icon to shrink the sidebar to an icon rail.
- **Content area** — summary cards and performance charts for the current day/period.

## Moving between pages

Use the sidebar to open each module. Each group can be expanded/collapsed. Pages not
available to your role are hidden.

:::tip Switch theme & language
Open the avatar menu → **Settings** to switch appearance (System / Light / Dark) and
language (Bahasa Indonesia / English). Your choice is saved in your browser.
:::
