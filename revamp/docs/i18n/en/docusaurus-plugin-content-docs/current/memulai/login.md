# Signing In

![SWAT sign-in page](/img/web/login.png)

1. Open the SWAT app address in your browser.
2. Enter the **username** and **password** provided by your administrator.
3. Click **Sign in**.

If the username or password is wrong, an error message appears directly under the
relevant field. Double-check capitalization and spaces.

## Changing your password on first sign-in

Some accounts are **required to change their password** on first sign-in for security.
When prompted, you'll be sent to the **Change Password** page — set a new password and
continue. You'll then land on the [dashboard](/memulai/dashboard).

## Forgot your password

Use the **Forgot password** link on the sign-in page to start recovery, or ask your
administrator to reset it.

:::note Permissions shape what you see
After signing in, the sidebar menus follow your account's **role**. Menus you don't have
permission for won't be shown. See [Roles & Permissions](/memulai/peran-akses).
:::
