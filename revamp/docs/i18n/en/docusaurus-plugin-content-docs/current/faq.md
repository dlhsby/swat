# FAQ & Help

### A menu I need isn't in the sidebar

Menus follow your account's **permissions**. If a module isn't shown, your role hasn't
been granted access to it. Contact your administrator — see [Roles &
Permissions](/memulai/peran-akses).

### I forgot my password

Use the **Forgot password** link on the sign-in page, or ask your administrator to reset
your password.

### I'm asked to change my password when signing in

That's normal for new accounts: a password change is **required** on first sign-in for
security. Once changed, you go straight to the dashboard.

### How do I change the language or theme?

Open the avatar menu (top right) → **Settings**. There you can choose the appearance
(System / Light / Dark) and language (Bahasa Indonesia / English).

### The data I see looks out of date

Reload the page. If it still looks off, note the steps you took and report them to your
administrator / technical team.

### Terms used in the app

- **Waste Hauling** — one hauling activity by a fleet vehicle (technical term: _Haul_).
- **Trip** — one route/round within a hauling (technical term: _Trip_).
- **Levy** — a charge for the service (Indonesian: _Retribusi_).
- **Disposal Permit** — a dumping permit/quota at the disposal site (Indonesian: _Jatah
  Kitir_).
